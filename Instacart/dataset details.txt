The Datasets present Customer, Products, orders and aisle related details
Each dataset have their own unique id 

#Datasets
aisle - The dataset has details of the aisle with an unique id
department - This dataset has the detail as which department the product belongs to
order_product and order_product_prior - Order details and Customers previous order details(reordered means customer 
ordered this product in both previous and current order)
order - order placed details
product - Product details with their aisle and department details
